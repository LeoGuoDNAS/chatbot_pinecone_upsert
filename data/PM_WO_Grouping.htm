PM WO Grouping

SAMPro uses a grouping order to determine whether or not PM Schedule lines will be combined on a single PM Work Order. By default, generating PMs combines Equipment onto WOs when the following list of values are matched up, one Client Site Equipment / PM Schedule line at a time.  If the next PM Schedule line has one or more fields that do not match the previous line evaluated, a new PM Work Order will be started.  

Site ID (from Client Site Equipment header)

Date Scheduled (from Client Site Equipment PM Schedule)

Job Cost Code (from Client Site Equipment PM Schedule)

Job Billing Item (from Client Site Equipment PM Schedule)

Escalation Profile (from Client Site Equipment PM Schedule or Client Site Equipment Header if PM Schedule is blank)

Technician (from Client Site Equipment PM Schedule or Client Site Equipment Header if PM Schedule is blank)

Account Manager (from Client Site Equipment header)

Dispatcher (from Client Site Equipment header)

Sales Rep (from Client Site Equipment header)

Service Account Manager (from Client Site Equipment header)

Master Contract Id (from the Client Site Equipment PM Schedule)

Work Code (unless overridden by registry option IgnoreCase3242 in sys-start-scrtepmwo. - from the Client Site Equipment PM Schedule. This registry generally does not apply if Master Contract is enabled as there is no way to assign work codes to specific equipment from Master Contract unless the Build Out functionality is in use))

Example:
Support Client Site ABC has three pieces of equipment scheduled for PM on June 1, 2021.  All three pieces of equipment have identical PM Schedule lines, but two have the Dispatcher (on the Service tab) set to George and the other set to Sally. In this case, two PM Work Orders would be generated: the two pieces of equipment with Dispatcher George would be on one, and the remaining equipment with Dispatcher Sally would be on the other.

The default grouping order for PM Work Order generation is listed above. This grouping order may be overridden by the orderby registry option in sys-start-scrtepmwo.

All of above are considered unless overridden by registry option singleequipmentperwo in sys-start-scrtepmwo. .
