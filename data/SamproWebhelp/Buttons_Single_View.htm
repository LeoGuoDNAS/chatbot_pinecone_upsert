Buttons - Magnify View

The Magnify (detail) View of child tab lines may be accessed by clicking the Magnify button. The following buttons enable the user to scroll though the lines in this child tab:

First – Previous – Next – Last line in the tab

In addition, Add, Insert and Delete lines buttons allow users to perform these functions in the Single (detail) view screens.

Clicking the Lines View button will return the tab to its initial, multiple line view.

Ctrl+Q: Toggles between the Magnify and Line views of the selected child tab line.
