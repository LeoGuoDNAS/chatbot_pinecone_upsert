Load Beginning Payroll Balances

Loading Payroll Y-T-D Beginning Balances By Employee

 

1. Setup a special PAY PERIOD for loading YTD beginning balances. DO NOT check Accrue to GL or PTO!

Example: PRHISTORY or BEGBAL

 

Try to use a clean cut-off - e.g. end of a PR quarter vs. mid quarter, as this will effect the Quarterly 941 reporting.

2. Prior to entering beginning balances, locate the reports which show the employer’s tax portions for FUTA and SUTA and the employees taxable earnings and exempt earnings for these. In addition, print the reports which show employee and employer FICA SS and FICA MC taxable earnings and exempt earnings (if over limit). If these reports cannot be located in your old system or from your payroll service, the amounts will have to be computed by the user.

3. Use the PAYROLL (ADJUSTMENT) JOURNAL to enter YTD beginning balances by EMPLOYEE. Click NEW and enter the Main Header info: Employee Id, Pay Period Id and click ADJUSTMENT. GL Account info and check# are not required.

Note: Leave the TOTALS (Total Earnings, Total Other, etc) and Net Pay boxes empty/blank until the Child TAB info has been entered. When finished entering Child tab info, enter the Total Earnings, Total Other, Total Tax and Total Deductions into the main header screen area and confirm Total NET PAY is total PAID to-date for this EMPLOYEE. If not, correct the appropriate child tab(s).

*** Warning *** Confirm data entry is correct and balances BEFORE clicking SAVE, as these transactions CAN NOT be EDITED !!!!

 

4. In P/R Journal, SKIP the EARNINGS TAB. On the HOURS TAB, enter total EARNINGS by Pay Type….

 

5. On the TAX TAB, enter the Taxes Withheld (Employee and Employer tax amounts)….

 

6. On the DEDUCTIONS TAB, enter the amounts for each type of deduction ….

 

7. Optional – enter WORKERS COMP tab info.

Note: Earnings TAB (skip) is used to enter FRINGES and PTO accruals and are not required.

8. When finished entering Child tab info, enter the Total Earnings, Total Other, Total Tax and Total Deductions into the main header screen area and confirm Total NET PAY is total PAID to-date for this EMPLOYEE. If not, correct the appropriate child tab(s).

*** Warning *** Confirm data entry is correct and balances BEFORE clicking SAVE, as these transactions CAN NOT be EDITED !!!!
