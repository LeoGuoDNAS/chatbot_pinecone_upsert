Client Site - Site Equipment Tab

Equipment Id: Name: Mfg: Model: S/N: Under Contract: Contract #:
This Query-View tab displays all active Client Site Equipment associated with this Site. You may double-click on an Equipment line to launch the Client Site Equipment viewer for that record.

With the UseMasterContractEquipmentTable global registry set to true, processing for the SiteEquipment list has changed slightly so that the Contract Start and End Dates are displayed and a Status column added.

Equipment Id:  Name:  Contract#: Starts:  Ends:   Mfg: Model: S/N:

This tab displays active Client Site Equipment associated with this Site. It also lists the Master Contract or Contracts the equipment is associated with. You may double-click on an Equipment line to launch the Client Site Equipment viewer for that record.

Status:

The Status column indicates the status of the Master Contract (if any) associated with the Equipment. If multiple contracts exist for a piece of equipment, multiple equipment lines may be displayed.

Active:  Today's date falls within the Master Contract Start and End Dates. If a Master Contract's Date Cancelled falls after today's date it will appear as Active.

Pending:  Today's date falls before the Master Contract Start Date.

NoContract: The equipment is not assigned to a Master Contract

Expired: The equipment is assigned to an expired Master Contract - today's Date falls after the End Date of the Master Contract.  Note that status filtering must be set to show Expired Contracts.

Cancelled: The equipment is assigned to a cancelled Master Contract - today's Date falls after the Cancelled Date of the Master Contract.  Note that status filtering must be set to show Cancelled Contracts.
