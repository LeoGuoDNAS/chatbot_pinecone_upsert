Line View Button

Many tabular views enable you to switch from the LineView (often requiring scrolling to view the right-hand fields) to a Detailed (single) view of one line.

Click the Line View button to return to the multiple line view.

To display the detailed view of a line, click the Magnify button in the first column of the line that you wish to display.

Ctrl+Q: Toggles between the Magnify and Line views of the selected child tab line.
