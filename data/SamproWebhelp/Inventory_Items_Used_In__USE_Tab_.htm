Inventory Items Where Used Tab

Purpose:

If this item is used in the manufacture of an other item (i.e., included on the other item's BOM tab), the item(s) it is used to make will appear on this screen.

Important Fields:

Inventory Item Id: Description: Quantity:
These display-only fields list the inventory items that this item is used in (is a component of). The quantity required for each item (or yield amount) is also displayed. These fields are maintained by the system when Bills of Materials are created or revised.
