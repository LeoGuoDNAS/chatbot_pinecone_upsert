Arkansas State Payroll Tax Table

Usage:

SAMPro tax table structure and formula are modeled after the federal tax tables.  The concept of a MINUS ADJUSTMENT used in the Arkansas formula is 180 degrees opposite of the federal formula.  Consequently, the AR published tables must be revised to convert the Minus Adjustment into a Plus Adjustment similar to the federal tables.
