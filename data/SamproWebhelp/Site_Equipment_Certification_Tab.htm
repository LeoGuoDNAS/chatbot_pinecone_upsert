Site Equipment Certification Tab

Purpose:

ThisÂ tab allows users to list Certifications required to work on the selected Equipment in addition to the Certification listed on the Equipment's Main tab and the Site's Certifications tab.

Usage:

When TechFinder is launched from the Dispatch Board or Work Order, all Certifications for the Site and the Certification tab of first piece of Equipment on the Work Order will be listed.

When TechFinder is launched from New Call, all Certifications for the selected Site and Equipment will be listed. This list is prepended with an ampersand & so that only those Techs with all the Certifications listed appear in TechFinder.

Important Fields:

Cert Id:
Enter a Certification Id (or double-click to select from a list) that should be added to this Equipment record.
