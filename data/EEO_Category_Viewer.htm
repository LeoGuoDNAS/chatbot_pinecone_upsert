EEO Category Viewer

Purpose:

This viewer enables you to specify the EEO Job Categories used by your company for reporting purposes.

Usage:

This field is referenced in the EEOC tab of the Employee viewer.

Important Fields:

Id:

The EEO Job Category code; all digits (including initial zeros) should be specified.

Description:

A brief description of this code.

Example (with 20digit codes):

Id Description

01  Officials and Managers

02  Professionals

03  Technicians

04  Sales Workers

05  Administrative Support Workers

06  Craft Workers (skilled)

07  Operatives (semi-skilled)

08  Laborers and Helpers (unskilled)

09  Service Workers
