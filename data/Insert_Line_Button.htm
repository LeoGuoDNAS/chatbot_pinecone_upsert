Insert/Copy Line Button

The Insert/Copy Line button is enabled in records that include multiple lines of data. When you press the Insert/Copy Line button, the system may ask you if you wish to copy the current line or simply insert a blank line above the current line. If you click Yes, the system will copy the selected line: you may edit it as needed. If you click No, a blank line will be inserted.

The ‘Copy’ portion of the Insert/Copy functionality is disabled in certain cases. For example, in the Work Order Labor Tab, imported lines may not be copied – but lines entered directly on the Labor tab may. If Copy is disabled, the system will simply insert a blank line.
