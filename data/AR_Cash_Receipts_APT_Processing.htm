AR Cash Receipts OpenEdge / Global Payments Processing

If you have established a MOTO account with OpenEdge / Global Payments, you may process a credit card payment or refund in the AR Cash Receipts Journal once your registry and servers have been correctly configured (see Open Edge Registry).

Note: Edits and deletions to AR lines for Credit Card paid entries are not allowed for Full users. Only Special (support) users are allowed to edit or delete. 

Processing Payments

Click Processing Credit Refunds for details on issuing credits.

1. If you use a different sweep account for OpenEdge payments, change the Bank Account on the GL tab.

2. On the Main tab, select Invoice or Invoices that the customer wishes to pay for.

3. Enter the Discount (if any) and Total Receipt; this is the amount that will be processed on the credit card.

4. Verify that the customer has their credit card ready, and then click the Process Payment button.

If your firm is set up for Payment Processing and an Invoice or Invoices have been selected for payment, the following happens when the Process Payments button is clicked:

Determine the Client Site associated with each Invoice that has been selected for payment in the transaction being recorded.

If an eligible alias token is NOT found, SAMPro will simply display a message reminding you that you will only have 60 seconds to enter the information; click Yes to continue.  

If one or more Aliases are found, the system will display a list of the eligible Payment Alias/Token values found in the above list of Sites.  To be eligible, a Site's Alias/Token must have an expiration date greater than or equal to the current system date. 

If No Selection is highlighted and the Select button clicked SAMPro will display a message reminding you that you will only have 60 seconds to enter the information; click Yes to continue.  Another message informs you that the journal entry will be added before Payment Processing; click Yes again.  

If an existing Alias is highlighted and the Select button clicked, the system will send the information to Open Edge; if approved, the Credit Card fields will be completed and the Cash Receipt saved.

If the Cancel or red X button is clicked, the list will be closed SAMPro will display a message reminding you that you will only have 60 seconds to enter the information; click Yes to continue or Cancel to opt out of the payment processing.

5. If an existing alias was not selected, SAMPro will display a message reminding you that you will only have 60 seconds to enter the information; click Yes to continue.  Another message inform you that the journal entry will be added before Payment Processing; click Yes again.  

6. When the X-Web or SAMPro's Credit Card Information for Tokenization portal opens, enter the customer's credit card number (without spaces or dashes) and expiration date and click Submit. If the Credit Card Information for Tokenization portal is being used, be sure that the Country field is completed before clicking Submit.

7. If the card is approved, the web portal will display "Approval" in red. If it is not approved, check the number and expiration date and try submitting it again.

In Open Edge / Global Payments, you can process a card (payment or alias) and get an Approval + an error attached. In previous releases, SAMPro treated this scenario as a failed transaction, but in reality the transaction had been approved and showed on the OpenEdge Merchant site if the Open Edge account was set up to approve transactions with these issues. SAMPro now treats the following as Approved:

Approved - AVS & CSC Mismatch
Approved - AVS Mismatch
Approved - CSC Mismatch

Note that an AVS Mismatch occurs if the billing address does not match the address on file with the card-issuing bank or credit card company.  A CSC Mismatch occurs if the Card Security Code does not match.  

8. When the payment is approved, the system will write the Approval Code back to the Check Number field, and complete the CC Masked Account field for future reference.

Timing Out: If the Alias is not generated within 60 seconds, it will not be written back to SAMPro, even if a success is noted in the web portal.  If this occurs, repeat the Alias process.

9. When preparing to enter the next Cash Receipt, remember to set the GL Bank Account to the appropriate value.

Note: For Cash Receipt Transactions, SAMPro will also send the SAMPro user ID to column "User ID" of export, and sent the User Def 1 to the Client Id.

Processing Credit Refunds

1. Select a Credit Memo in the AR Cash Receipts Journal, and enter the Total Receipt as a negative number.

2. Verify that the customer has their credit card ready, and then click the Process Payment button.

3. Click through the usual messages; when the x-web portal opens, enter the customer's credit card number (without spaces or dashes) and expiration date and click Submit.

4. If the card is approved, the web portal will display "Approval" in red and the customer's card will be credited with the refund.  If it is not approved, check the number and expiration date and try submitting it again.
