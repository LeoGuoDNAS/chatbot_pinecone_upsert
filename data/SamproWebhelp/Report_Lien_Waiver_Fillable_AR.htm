Report Lien Waiver Fillable AR

SAMPro's Fillable Form functionality allows an AR Lien Waiver to generated as a fillable PDF.  This uses the boilerplate information included in a standard AR Lien Waiver to be combined with the information from SAMPro to quickly and easily generate the document. This Lien Waiver may then be emailed to the customer when payment is received. The Conditional option of the report indicates the current payment on the Job, and allows disputed amounts to be identified.  The Final option provides the formal release of the lien in and would be provided when the work is complete and all payments have been received.

Important: Note that this feature requires use of the XmlToPdf[2019-01-16 Java 8-11].jar file (or higher).  

Registry:

See Registry Report Lien Waiver (AR) for the registry and template setup required for this report.

Usage:

A Job needs to be marked as Lien Waiver Required to be included on this report.

The Through Date on the Conditional Progress Payment form is populated from the Job record's Billed Through Date.

The following information from the database will be used when you when you run the report:

Name of Claimant and Check Payable To: CompanyName (from the Company record (multi-company) or the companyname registry

Name of Customer and Maker of Check: Client Name

Job Location: Site Address

Amount of Check: Net Invoice

Additional fields will be completed on the report range screen as listed below.

Important Fields:

Beginning Date: Ending Date:
This Date range must include the Date Invoiced of the invoice to be included on the report.

Lien Waiver Limit:
The Invoice Amount of the Invoice the waiver is being issued for needs to be greater than or equal the  Lien Waiver Limit.

Owner:
Enter the property Owner's name as it should be printed on the report.

Claimant Title:
The Claimant's title in your Company as it should appear on the report. This will appear below the Claimant's Signature when the fillable form is generated.

Conditional Progress - Date(s) of Waiver and Release
Enter the appropriate date(s) as they should appear on the Conditional version of the report.

Conditional Progress - Unpaid Progress Payments
Enter the total Amount Unpaid as it should appear on the Conditional version of the report.

Conditional Final - Document Does Not Affect the Following
Description of any exceptions, i.e. "C.O.'s 1 thru 3".

Conditional Final - Disputed Claims for Extras
Total amount of disputed claims.

Lien Waiver: Conditional Progress  -  Conditional Final

Select Conditional Progress if the Job is partial billed.

Select Conditional Final if the Job if final billed.

 Click the Go button to start the report.

Report Viewer

When the report is first run, the information that will be inserted in the fillable form template will appear.

If the information that appears in the report preview appears correct, click the Fillable Form button to generate the formatted, PDF version of the report. The PDF will be displayed when it has been opened (unless disabled via registry).
