Job Billing Item Status

Purpose:

This record may be used to specify user-definable status codes for Job Billing Items. Typically, this would be used for Change Order type Billing Items for tracking purposes. For example, statuses could include New, Submitted, Review, Approved.

Usage:

Referenced in: Job Billing Item Viewer

This record has NO meaning in SAMPRO.  It is strictly for use on custom reports designed by users.

Important Fields:

Status Id:
Uniquely defines this Job Billing Status record.

Status Description:
Briefly describes this status.
