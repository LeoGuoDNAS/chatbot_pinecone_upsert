941S Report with FFCRA Covid 19 Support

Purpose:

This report is designed to assist the user in completing the Employer’s Quarterly Federal Tax Return which includes Covid related information (if applicable). This report is generated with Payroll History data as well as user values entered on the Report Range screen.

Usage:

The updated PR 941 Quarterly Summary (RPR941S.rpt) report dated July 28, 2020 or later supports the modifications discussed in the COV 19 SAMPro Notice. SAMPro Version 7.3 or higher is required.

Registry:

See COV-19 SAMPRO Notice for the required registry and data setup needed to support the COVID-19 Families First Coronavirus Response Act (FFCRA) fields.

The revised 941 report has been modified to utilize the 3 new Pay Type User fields to determine the following values for these new lines on the form. If these procedures are followed, the following fields will be completed on the printed report:

5a - Taxable SS Wages - excludes the new amounts in columns 5a  (i) and (ii) 

5a.i – Qualified Sick Leave – see CUSTOM Pay Type User fields:    CV-SELF and CV-PTO

5a.ii - Qualified Family Leave – see CUSTOM Pay Type User fields:   CV-FML

Important Fields: Report Range Screen

PR Year: PR Quarter:
The year and quarter you wish to run this report for.

(5b) Social Security Tips
SAM Pro does not track Social Security Tips. This data entry field may be completed by the user if Social Security Tips should be reported.

(8) FICA Sick Pay Adjustment.
(9) FICA Other Pay Adjustment
Adjustments (not reflected in PR History) to FICA Sick / Other Pay for this quarter should be entered in these fields.

(11a) Qualified Small Business Research Credit:

Credit Qualified small business payroll tax credit for increasing research activities.

(11b) Nonrefundable Qualified Sick/Family Leave Credit:

Nonrefundable portion of credit for qualified sick and family leave wages from Worksheet 1.

(11c) Nonrefundable Employee Retention Credit:

Nonrefundable portion of employee retention credit from Worksheet 1.

(13a) Total Deposits, Including Prior Quart Overpayments:

Total deposits for this quarter, including overpayment applied from a prior quarter and overpayments applied from Form 941-X, 941-X (PR), 944-X, or 944-X (SP) filed in the current quarter.

(13b) Deferred Employer Social Security Tax:

Deferred amount of the employer share of social security tax.

(13c) Refundable Family Sick/Family Leave Credit:

Refundable portion of credit for qualified sick and family leave wages from Worksheet 1.

(13d) Refundable Employee Retention Credit:

Refundable portion of employee retention credit from Worksheet.

(13f) Total Advances Received (Form 7200):

Total advances received from filing Form(s) 7200 for the quarter.

Note: The above lines will be used to calculate the various totals to determine the Balance Due on line 14 or Overpayment on line 15 of the printed report as well as 13e and 13g.

Description:
Enter any text that you wish to appear on the report header in this field.
