Refrigerant Leak Action Viewer

Purpose:

Refrigerant Tracking is required by the EPA. TechAnywhere supports the entry of data so that the necessary reports can be run. Alternately, data may be entered directly into the Refrigerant Tracking History viewer. The Refrigerant Leak Action record describes what the technician did.

Related Records:

Important Fields:

Leak Action Id:
This field uniquely identifies this action (e.g., Repair-CFM).

Description:
This field provides a longer description of the action (e.g., Repair-Condenser Fan Motor).

Leak Action User1-2:
These fields may be used to record additional information regarding this action.
