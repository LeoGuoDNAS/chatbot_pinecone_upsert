Available Hours View of the Technician Status Summary

Version 7.3+:

The Technician Status Summary of the Dispatch Board can be toggled between the standard view (the technician's current location) and the Available Technician Hours View. When you click the Available Technician Hours button, the Technician Status Summary will toggle to the Available Hours view, which shows the number of hours available for scheduling based on an 8-hour day (or as specified via Registry: see Registry Dispatch Board (sys-viewer-vdsptchbrd).

Version 7.3+:

Clicking the pushed (down) Available Technician Hours button will toggle the Technician Status Summary back to its standard view.

The Available Hours viewer provides a tabular, day-by-day view of the hours available for each technician. This information is displayed in the following columns:

Tech ID  The technician's ID
Calls   The total number of calls assigned for this date range
Day/Date  The total hours available on this day for this technician


Example
 Suppose a "4.00" appears in the Day/Date column for technician GARIBALDI on June 20. Assuming a standard 8-hour day, he may be scheduled for up to 4 additional hours for this day.

A blank in the Day/Date column means that the technician is fully scheduled for that day (8 hours have already been assigned). A negative number in the Day/Date column means that a the technician is overbook for that day. For example, if a "2-" appears, the technician is scheduled for 10 hours that day - a two hour overload. The "ALL" line displays the sum of available hours for all technicians on the board. Inactive technician will show blank (or negative) hours.

Since the Available Hours View is designed to display possible openings (holes) where a Work Order can be scheduled today or in the future, it is implemented to work with the following Date Ranges:

 Today   Tomorrow  Tomorrow + prior to Tomorrow
 This Week  This Week + Prior
 Next Week  Next Week + Prior
 This Week + Next Week  This Week+Next Week+Prior
 This Month  Next Month

In the cases of This Week and This Month, Available Hours from the current date onward will be shown, for Next Week and Prior, only Next Week will appear in the Available hours, and so on.

Changing the Date/Technician Scheduled in the Available Hours View
1. Verify that the Available Hours view appears in the upper half.

2. Click on the dispatch on the lower half of the board that you wish to reschedule to a different tech/day. The work order list may be in either graphics or text mode.

3. With the left mouse button depressed, drag the dispatch from the lower half of the board up to the desired cell (Day/Date column and Technician row) of the Available Hours table in the upper portion of the screen and then release the mouse button.

4. The system will ask you to confirm the charge; press Yes and the dispatch will be rescheduled/reassigned. You will be warned if you assign a call to an inactive technician.
