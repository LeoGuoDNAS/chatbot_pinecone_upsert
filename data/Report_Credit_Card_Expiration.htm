Report Credit Card Expiration

This report (RCCEXPRTN.rpt) is designed to alert users to upcoming Credit Card expiration dates in the Client Site for payment processing. The report lists the Site Id and Name, Contact Name and Phone Number, Masked Credit Number and Expiration Date for each Site with an expired Credit Card.

Start Date:   End Date:
At least one of this fields must be completed.

If the Credit Card Expiration Date falls on or after the Start Date, the Site will be included on the report.  

If the Credit Card Expiration Date falls on or before the End Date, the Site will be included on the report.

These ranges permit Sites to be filtered by the Site Id or Account Manager.
