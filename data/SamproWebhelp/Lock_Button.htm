Change Password  Button

 

The Lock/Change Password button enables the user to change his or her user password.

1. To change your password, click the button. Enter your new password in the New Password field. Note: your password must conform to the minimum length and format constraints defined by your firm. Refer to your company's internal documentation or database administration for details.

2. Re-enter your password in the Confirm Password field. If you did not correctly do so, the system will alert you to try again.

3. Once you have successfully entered and confirmed your new password, click the OK button.
