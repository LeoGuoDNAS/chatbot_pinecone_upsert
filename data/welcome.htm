Support Tips

Topics listed in the Support Tips book (see the TOC pane to the left) provide a variety of useful information and support suggestions. To contribute additional ‘tips’ to support help, send Jean a Word document with the text and screen captures (if any), as well as suggestions for key words.

Registry Entries

Note that support-only options are incorporated in the main registry topics. These options are identified by dark red text, and will only be displayed in versions of help compiled for support. They will not be displayed in help compiled for customers.

Implementations

Implementation notes (by our internal customer number) that you would like included in support help should be e-mailed to Jean.
