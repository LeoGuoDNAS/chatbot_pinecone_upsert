Change Technician Button

Version 7.3+:

The Change Technician button enables you to change or assign a technician to the Work Order selected in the lower pane of the Dispatch Board. First, verify that a Work Order is selected in the lower pane of the Dispatch window. Next, click on the Change Technician button to display a list of active technicians in the upper pane. Double-Click on the technician to whom you wish to assign/reassign the work order.
