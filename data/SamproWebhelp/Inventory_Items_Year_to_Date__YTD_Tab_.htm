Inventory Item YTD (Year to Date) Tab

Purpose:

This information-only screen lists the current and previous Year to Date sales figures for this item. To view detailed information by location for a specific year, click on a line.

Important Fields: Initial Screen

Fiscal Year
 The Fiscal Year summarized on this line.

Quantity Used
The number of items used in manufacturing.

Quantity Sold
The number of items sold as finished goods.

Cost YTD
The cost YTD of making these items.

Sales YTD
The dollar amount sold.

Received YTD
The dollar amount actually received.
