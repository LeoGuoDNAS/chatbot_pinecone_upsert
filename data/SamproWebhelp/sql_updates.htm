Cash Receipt Fixes

If you making SQL changes to fix "Cash Receipt" info, you may also need to fix the info in table:  ARGLHSTRY

Accounts Payable

If you’re making SQL changes to fix Accts Payable problems with AP Journal or AP Checks, be sure to check/fix the info in table APGLHSTRY (and VNDRINVCE, as needed).

7.2 – AR GL History and AP GL History

In 7.2 - programming added new VIEWERS to VIEW the info in tables ARGLHSTRY & APGLHSTRY –  where before they were just used to print the info on the AP-GL Audit Report and AR-GL Audit Report. The data in these tables needs to mirror what was processed.  The purpose of these tables are to store the INVOICE AMOUNT and RETAINAGE AMOUNTS and PAYMENT AMOUNTS to compare with what hit AR/AP vs what hit GL and determine if an out of balance exists.
