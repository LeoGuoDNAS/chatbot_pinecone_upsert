Refrigerant Leak Location Viewer

Purpose:

Refrigerant Tracking is required by the EPA. TechAnywhere supports the entry of data so that the necessary reports can be run. Alternately, data may be entered directly into the Refrigerant Tracking History viewer. The Refrigerant Leak Location record describes where in the equipment the leak occurred.

Important Fields:

Leak Location Id:
This field uniquely identifies this Location (e.g., DL-BallValve).

Description:
This field provides a longer description of the location (e.g., Discharge Line-Ball Valve).

Leak Location User1-2:
These fields may be used to record additional information regarding this location.
