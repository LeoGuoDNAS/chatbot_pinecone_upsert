In the past, the decision was made that the system would only update the WO Escalation status if an Escalation Profile existed in the system (regardless of whether or not escalation manager was being used) for performance reasons. For a variety of reasons (including invoicing and credit hold processing), the WO Escalation status/Status Category should be updated for all users. The following registry option results in the work order escalation status always being updated, regardless of whether or not an Escalation Profile exists.

Id: sys-ffv-global-settings
Setting Key: EscalationManager Setting Value: always
