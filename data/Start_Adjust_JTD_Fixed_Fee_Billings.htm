Start Adjust JTD Fixed Fee Billings

Purpose:

This starter function allows adjustments to be made to previously billed Contract Amounts for the selected Job. All adjustments must net to zero. When you add the adjustment, a new AR Sales Journal entry will be generated.

Initial Screen

Job Id:
Enter the Job Id of the Job you wish to adjust.  The Invoice Status of the Job will be displayed when you select the Job; the Job must have an Invoice Status of Hold or Posted in order to proceed with this function. 

Click Go to continue; if the selected Job has no JTD entries eligible for adjustment, or if the Invoice Status is not set to Hold or Posted, the user will be notified and will not be allowed to proceed.

Adjust JTD Fixed Fee BiIling Screen

Main Tab

Job Id - Client Id - Invoice Id
These display-only fields identify the Job being adjusted.

Invoice Date:
The Invoice Date of the adjustment; it will be used to set the Invoice Date in the AR Sales Journal generated when this adjustment is added.

Period Id:   Effective Date:
The Accounting Period and Effective Date the adjustment will be posted to. The Default Accounting period and today's date will default but may be adjusted by the user.

Tax Group Id:
The field defaults from the Job's Tax Group.

GL Entity:
This field defaults from Job's GL Entity.

GL Tab:

AR Control:

GL Account Id:  GL Entity Id:
These fields default from the Job record's corresponding AR Control GL Account and GL Entity fields.

GL Currency Adjustment:

GL Account:    GL Entity:
If a Currency Id is specified for the Job's Client, this field defaults from the Currency Master record.

Prices Tab:

Cost Code - Billing Item - GL Account - GL Entity - Revenue Type
These fields cannot be changed for existing lines which were defaulted by the system.  

The Add Line button may be used to add adjustment lines.

Contract Tot
This field displays the Contract Total of the Billing Item or Change Order specified on the line.

CI-JCH:
The Contract Invoiced thus far, from Job History.

CI-NEW;   CI-CHNGE:
If you want to adjust the Contract Invoiced amount, either enter the CI-NEW amount as you wish it to appear with the adjustment, or enter the CI-CHNGE field with the amount of the adjustment itself. The system will calculate the other field for you.

Example: Reduce the Amount Invoiced by $10 on one line:

Remember that your adjustments must net to zero dollars in order to save your changes.

When you Add this record, a zero dollar AR Sales Journal entry will be generate, implementing your adjustments to the selected Job.
