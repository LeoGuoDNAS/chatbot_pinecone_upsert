Clock Viewer

Purpose:

A clock that easily allows you to set a time field in digital or analog mode.

Usage:

Double-click (or press Alt+F7) on a time field to access the Clock. By default, the current time (as set on your system) is selected when you open the clock viewer. If the clock viewer remains open, the time will not increment. If you close and then reopen the clock viewer, the new current time will be selected.

You can enter the current time without accessing the Clock by simply pressing Ctrl+b.

There are two ways of resetting the time:

Digital
You may increment or decrement the time on the clock viewer by clicking the HOUR or MIN (minute) buttons.

Analog
Alternately, you may use the drag and drop feature to reset the analog (round) clock. To set the time forward (clockwise), click and drag the hour or minute hand to the desired location. To set the time back (counterclockwise), depress the Ctrl key, and then click and drag the hour or minute hand to the desired location. If the Ctrl key is not depressed throughout this operation, the system will interpret the drag and drop as setting the clock ahead!

Note: Minutes are set in five-minute increments in both digital and analog modes. If you need to set a time to an exact minute (or second), enter the exact (24 hour) time in the Time field in the upper left-hand section of the clock viewer.

Important Buttons:

When the time is correctly selected, click the Set Value button located on the side button bar. You will automatically return the selected time to the viewer from which you accessed the Calendar.
