Vendor Additional Contacts Tab

This tab is used to record the detailed contact information for this vendor. Typically, it is used in conjunction with the Service Provider Applet to provide a flexible range of contact information for subcontractor vendors.

Important Fields:

Contact Type Id:
Enter a valid Contact Type Id (or double-click to select from a list). This field defines what type of contact is being listed for reporting and informational purposes.

First Name: Last Name:
The First and Last Name of the contact.

Salutation:
Mr., Mrs., Ms., Miss.

Title:
Project Manager, Billing Clerk, etc.

Phone: Ext: Fax Phone: Cell Phone:
The voice and fax phone numbers for this individual.
