SIC Viewer

Purpose:

This viewer enables you to enter and maintain SIC (Standard Industrial Classification) codes.

Usage:

SIC codes are used in conjunction with the Job record for reporting purposes.

Important Fields.

SIC Id:
Required. A SIC (standard industrial classification code) code. This field is ten characters in length to support the extended SIC classifications.

SIC Description:
Briefly describes this SIC code.
