California Supplemental Sick Leave

California recently passed Senate Bill 95 which went into Effective March 29, 2021 for California employers with over 25 employees to provide to their workers 80 hours of COVID-19 related Supplemental Paid Sick Leave.  It is effective from Jan 1 thru Sept 30, 2021.   See the following Pay Type screen that users are advised to setup as it must be tracked separately on the Pay Stubs.   They must also enter each employees balance using the new Pay Type.  The CA SPSL balance can be entered 2 ways, either on the Employees PTO tab OR make a PR Adjustment to the Hours tab using “Hours Acc”.   Any unused balance will have to be remove with another adjustment when the program expires.

Note:   Customers can use any Pay Type “ID”  they want, however they should add that custom user field (see 941S Covid Registry and Setup) on their pay type as:  CV-SPSL for any potential future use.  However, no additional programming or reporting needs have yet been identified as being required.
