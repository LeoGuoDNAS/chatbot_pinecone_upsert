Invoice Master Statuses

New – If an invoice number is required for whatever invoicing function, the screen initially makes a new record and marks it as New – It may remain in this state for a very brief period or may stay if there was a "meltdown" of the PC and it didn’t go to a new state.

Computed – Construction job primarily uses this state through the function "Record Billing Adjustments", but can be very briefly used in service compute – the invoice is in a state of computed, not printed, not posted.

Posted – Invoice was computed, printed, and posted.

Reversed – Original Invoice that was printed and posted was reversed.

Unknown – Service Invoice computed, may or may not be printed, but was not posted.
