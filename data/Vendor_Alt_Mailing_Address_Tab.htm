Vendor Alt Mailing Address Tab

The Alt Mailing Address tab displays a list of all Vendor Mail-To addresses associated with this Vendor. New entries can be made to this tab by clicking the button on the Main tab of the Vendor viewer (you will have to score down a bit to see it. This button enables you to create a new Mail-To record for this Vendor, and automatically adds it to the Alternate Mail Address tab in the lower section of the Vendor viewer. When you click this button, the system will open the Vendor Mail-To viewer; the Vender Id will be defaulted to the current Vendor. Complete the remaining fields, and click the Add Record button.
