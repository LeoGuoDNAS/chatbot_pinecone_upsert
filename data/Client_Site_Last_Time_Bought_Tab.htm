Client Site Last Time Sold Tab

This tab lists the most recent COP sales and no-charge samples sent to this site by inventory id.

The Client Order viewer provides for a mechanism in which item 'A' is sold to a client but that item is labeled on the invoice as item 'B'. Item 'B' is known as the 'substitute inventory item'. The information on this tab reflects the substitute inventory item specified on the Client Order rather than on the actual inventory item. NOTE: unless specifically entered differently on the Client Order, the actual and substitute items are identical.

You may double-click on a line to view order detail.

The following fields are displayed: 

Inventory Item Number 

Description

COP Order Number

Quantity

Unit of Measure

Unit Price

Unit Repack

Ship Date

% on Time
