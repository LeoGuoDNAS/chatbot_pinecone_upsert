Registry Earnings Group

Registries to Earnings By End Date

In previous releases of version 9.1, the Earnings Group child tab was automatically sorted in descending order by the End Date of each child line. Due to issues maintaining complex Earnings Groups, the default behavior has been changed back so that the lines are NOT resorted when the record is saved. If you wish to have the child lines sorted, the following registry must be implemented. This functionality is also available for the Employee and Deduction Group viewers.

Id: sys-viewer-vernngsgrp

Setting Key: NoSortOnChildren Â Setting Value: false (default is true to prevent sorting)
